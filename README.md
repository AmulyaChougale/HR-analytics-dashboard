#  HR ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

An interactive and visually optimized Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee demographics, attrition patterns, job satisfaction, and workforce distribution.

##  Purpose
The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a clear, data-driven view of workforce behavior, enabling HR leaders to:
- Identify attrition drivers
- Understand demographic patterns
- Evaluate job satisfaction trends
- Support informed decision-making in recruitment planning, employee retention, and engagement strategy development

##  Tech Stack
- Power BI Desktop – Dashboard development and visualization
- Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Measures for KPIs and attrition metrics
- Data Modeling – Relationships for cross-filtered HR analysis
- Excel (.xlsx) – Source dataset

##  Data Source
### HR Data.xlsx
Employee-level HR dataset containing fields such as:
- Age, Gender, Education Field
- Job Role, Department
- Attrition Status
- Monthly Income
- Job Satisfaction & Environment Satisfaction
- Work-Life Balance
- Years at Company

##  Features
###  Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to understand the root causes of:
- Employee attrition
- Workforce imbalance
- Engagement gaps

###  Goal of the Dashboard
- Monitor attrition trends
- Understand workforce demographics
- Evaluate satisfaction levels across job roles
- Support decisions in workforce planning, retention, and training

### Key Visuals Overview
- KPI Cards: Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Average Age, Average Salary
- Gender & Age Band Distribution: Donut charts showing gender breakdown across age groups
- Attrition by Department: Pie chart displaying turnover across R&D, Sales, and HR
- Education Field Breakdown: Bar chart showing distribution of employees by education
- Job Role Distribution: Comparison of employee counts across different job roles
- Job Satisfaction Matrix: Table showing satisfaction scores across roles

##  Business Impact & Insights
- Identify departments and roles with higher attrition
- Understand demographic trends for better recruitment planning
- Recognize satisfaction gaps to improve employee engagement
- Support HR strategy with evidence-based insights
